About DJ Angerone

Mike Myers, professionally known as DJ Angerone, is internationally acclaimed Progressive House & Trance DJ & musician.

I try to experiment with various music genres in every track that I make.

Since the beginning of his career in 2010, he has already release, 5 EPs on a range of waldwide known labels (Booster Records, DSTRCT Recordings etc.) and received a number of music awards.
